POP!_OS BudgetGamers Edition

A POP!_OS Based Linux Distro with KDE rather than the MACOS Style Desktop Environment.

Download ISO
NOTE: Hardware requirements are subject to change due to the current experimental nature of the distro.

System Requirements

Minimum

  • OS: 64-bit
  • Processor: Early Ryzen or Intel Equivalent
  • Memory: 6GB RAM
  • Graphics: Integrated Graphics
  • Storage: 64GB HDD available space

Recommended

  • OS: 64-bit
  • Processor: Ryzen 3000 or Intel 7th gen equivalent
  • Memory: 16GB RAM
  • Graphics: Dedicated Graphics Card
  • Storage: 256GB SSD available space

* Hardware Requirements are subject to user preference.
